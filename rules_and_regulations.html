Rules and Regulations

1. Respect all members of the society.
2. Maintain sportsmanship during gameplay.
3. Follow the instructions of society moderators and administrators.
4. Any form of harassment or discrimination is strictly prohibited.
5. Ensure fair play and adhere to the established code of conduct.
6. Violation of rules may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion from the society.
